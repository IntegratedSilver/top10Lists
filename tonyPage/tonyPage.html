Top 10 Video Games


10. Red Dead Redemption 2 (2018)

Red Dead Redemption 2 was developed and published by Rockstar Games, and a prequel to the first Red Dead Redemption. RDR2 is a action-adventure game.



9. Super Mario 64 (1996)

Super Mario 64 was developed and published by Nintendo, and is the first 3D Mario game and served as an example for the next games in the series. Super Mario 64 is a platformer game.



8. Halo Reach (2010)

Halo: Reach was developed by Bungie and published by Microsoft Game Studios, and sixth installment in the Halo series and a direct prequel to Halo: Combat Evolved. Halo: Reach is a first-person shooter.



7. Minecraft (2011)

Minecraft was devaloped and published by Mojang Studios. Though originally on PC, it has made it’s way onto ever platform imaginable, ranging from PC to gaming consoles and even to mobile phones. Minecraft is a sandbox game.



6. Wii Sports (2006)

Wii Sports was developed and published by Nintendo. Wii Sports made use of the Wii’s unique TV remote style controller and the motion controls that came with it. Wii sports is a sports game.



5. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (2014)

Mario Kart 8 was developed and published by Nintendo. Mario Kart 8 features a huge roster of characters from across the Mario Series has a wide variety of stages to choose from. Mario Kart 8 is a racing game.



4. Fortnite (2017)

Fortnite was developed by Epic Games. It’s unique “build” style made it extremely popular and has made it’s way onto every platform imaginable. Fortnite is a battle royale, third-person shooter.



3. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (2018)

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate was developed by Bandai Namco Studios and Sora Ltd. and published by Nintendo. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is an amazing crossover game featuring many well known characters from Nintendo, Sega, Capcom, Konami, Square Enix, Bandai Namco and many more. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a platform fighting game.



2. Grand Theft Auto V (2013)

Grand Theft Auto 5 was developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is the seventh main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series, following 2008's Grand Theft Auto IV. Grand Theft Auto 5 is a action-adventure game.



1. Spiderman 2 (2023)

Spider-Man 2 was developed by Insomniac Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Spider-Man 2 features new in game quality of life improvements, new story, and new playable characters. Spider-Man 2 is a action-adventure game.
