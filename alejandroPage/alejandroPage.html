Top 10 Video Game Consoles


10. Nintendo Entertainment System (1985)

The Nintendo Entertainment System, also known as Famicom, is apart of the third generation of consoles. It’s groundbreaking technology was a must have for all households at the time. Some titles on the NES include Super Mario Bros., Final Fantasy, and The Legend of Zelda.




9. Sega Dreamcast (1998)

The Sega Dreamcast was the first of the sixth generation of consoles. The Dreamcast displayed an advancement of graphics compared to the previous generation. Some titles on the Dreamcast include Sonic Adventure, Soulcalibur, and Jet Set Radio.





8. Nintendo DS (2004)

The Nintendo DS is apart of the seventh generation of consoles. It’s dual screen display set itself apart from the competition at the time in terms of uniqueness, comfort, and portability. To this day, the DS has the the second most amount of total units sold. Some titles on the DS include Pokemon Platinum, Mario Kart DS, and Animal Crossing Wild World.





7. PlayStation 1 (1994)

The PlayStation was apart of the fifth generation of consoles. The PS1 set what would later be the standard for gaming controller designs. Games on the PS1 include Final Fantasy VII, Metal Gear Solid, and Castlevania: Symphony of the Night.





6. Xbox 360 (2005)

The Xbox 360 is apart of the seventh generation of consoles. The Xbox 360 is well remembered for it’s online multiplayer capability for the time. Games on the Xbox 360 include Halo 3, Gears of War, and The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim.





5. Nintendo Switch (2017)

The Nintendo Switch is apart of the eighth generation of consoles. The Switch is the fist hybrid console, being able to be docked and displayed on a TV or taken on the go and used at any moment. Games on the Switch include Xenoblade Chronicles 2, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, and Octopath Traveler.





4. PlayStation Portable (2004)

The PlayStation Portable is apart of the seventh generation of consoles. The PSP is a handheld console that displayed amazing graphical ability for such a compact system. Games on the PSP include Monster Hunter Freedom Unite, Persona 3 Portable, and Kingdom Hearts: Birth by Sleep.





3. PlayStation 2 (2000)

The PlayStation 2 is apart of the sixth generation of consoles. To this day, the PS2 has the most units sold compared to every other console in history, having over 155 million units sold. Games on the PS2 include Final Fantasy X, Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, and Guitar Hero II.





2. Nintendo Wii (2006)

The Nintendo Wii is apart of the seventh generation of consoles. The Wii had an interesting controller gimmick being the Wii Remote. The Wii was meant to simulate a TV and a TV remote. Games on the Wii include Xenoblade Chronicles, Mario Galaxy and Kirby Return to Dream Land.





1. PlayStation 5 (2020)

The PlayStation 5 is apart of the ninth generation of consoles. The PS5 has the best graphics out of any console so far. Games on the PS5 include, Demon’s Souls, Spider-Man 2, and Final Fantasy XVI.
